Zombie, Run! Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do you move around the map?
A: Grab your device, and go outside. The blue dot representing your location is your actual location, based on your GPS device. You navigate around the map by walking or running, with your feet.

Q: Why isn't my device successfully finding my location?
A: The Zombie, Run! device needs to know your exact location, and uses the GPS position system to determine where you are. As it is based on satellite signals, GPS works best when you have a clear view of a large portion of the sky. We have found that the Zombie, Run! device works best away from tall buildings or overhanging foliage. Heavy cloud cover can also influence the zombie finder's effectiveness. Urban settings and forested areas are some of the more dangerous areas to be in during a zombie infestation, and we recommend that you avoid those areas on principle.
