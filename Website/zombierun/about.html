Zombie, Run!™ Zombie Finder.

When living in a zombie-infested city, one needs a little help to maintain a normal, healthy, not-undead existence. The Zombie, Run!™ Zombie Finder is an important surivival tool that helps you maintain your normal routine and previous happy lifestyle. The Zombie, Run!™ Zombie Finder shows you the locations of nearby zombies, helping you safely navigate an infested area.

Basic instructions for use: When out running an errand or for a walk, start the zombie finder, wait for the tool to acquire your location, indicate your intended destination, and safely navigate to your destination.

Markers:

  • Blue Dot: This is your current location.
  • Flag: This is your destination, for ease of reference.
  • Green Zombies: These are zombies who are unaware of your location, and are simply meandering about, as zombies do.
  • Red Zombies: These are zombies who have been alerted to your location, and are hastily making their way towards your tasty, tasty brains. Try to ensure that you make more haste than they do, or you will soon be waste. Remember: make haste, not waste.
